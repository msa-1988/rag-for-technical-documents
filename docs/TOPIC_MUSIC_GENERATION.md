# Topic Pack: AI Music Generation

Current public dataset for the first RAG build:
`AI Music Generation`

## Why this topic works well

This is a strong first public dataset because it gives you:
- a creative and memorable demo topic
- public papers you can safely use
- a mix of surveys and model papers
- enough overlap and contrast for real retrieval questions

It also makes the RAG app more interesting than a generic chatbot over random PDFs.

## Selected Papers

1. `A Survey of AI Music Generation Tools and Models`
   - broad overview of tools, categories, and limitations
   - arXiv: `2308.12982`

2. `Applications and Advances of Artificial Intelligence in Music Generation: A Review`
   - newer review of technologies, datasets, evaluation, and applications
   - arXiv: `2409.03715`

3. `MusicLM: Generating Music From Text`
   - major text-to-music paper from Google
   - arXiv: `2301.11325`

4. `Simple and Controllable Music Generation`
   - MusicGen paper from Meta
   - arXiv: `2306.05284`

5. `Controllable Music Production with Diffusion Models and Guidance Gradients`
   - practical control and editing focus
   - arXiv: `2311.00613`

6. `Fast Timing-Conditioned Latent Audio Diffusion`
   - Stable Audio paper, useful for long-form and variable-length generation
   - arXiv: `2402.04825`

## Why this paper mix is good

This set gives you:
- `2` survey/review papers for broad grounding
- `2` major text-to-music model papers
- `2` diffusion/control papers for deeper comparisons

That means your app can answer:
- definition questions
- architecture questions
- comparison questions
- synthesis questions

## Good First Questions

- What are the main categories of AI music generation systems?
- How do MusicLM and MusicGen differ?
- What does controllability mean in AI music generation?
- What are common evaluation challenges in this field?
- Which papers focus on editing existing music rather than generating from scratch?
- Which papers discuss long-form or variable-length audio generation?

## Good Comparison Questions

- Compare autoregressive and diffusion-based approaches for music generation.
- Compare MusicLM, MusicGen, and Stable Audio in terms of control and generation strategy.
- What themes appear in both review papers?
- Which limitations are repeated across multiple papers?

## Good Demo Questions For Recruiters

- If I were building a startup product in AI music generation, what features would matter most?
- What are the biggest gaps between creative control and output quality?
- Which approach looks most practical for real-world music production workflows?
